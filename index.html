About Zephyr

Initiated in 2013, Zephyr has emerged as the biggest college based fiesta and as one of the most awaited and anticipated cultural event of the Chaibasa Engineering college. With it's exciting competitions, splendid performances and glamorous pro-nites, Zephyr is one of the largest cultural festival inviting a huge crowd of students from different colleges. Over the years, Zephyr has seen many outstanding talents and a lot of scintillating performances. Let's all get together and see what surprises Zephyr has up its sleeves this time. The festival is celebrated for hosting over many diverse events, including three professional shows that have featured musical luminaries such as TrapBand and professional concerts. It showcases a wide array of genres, including Rock, Classical, EDM, and Pop. This year marks the 5th edition of Zephyr, scheduled to be held offline from January onwards. Following last year's success, which drew a global audience, this year's event is anticipated to harness even greater potential and deliver an unforgettable experience.
